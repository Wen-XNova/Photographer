About

With over a decade of experience behind the lens, Jordan Lee has built a reputation for capturing authentic, emotionally resonant images that tell a story. Based in Toronto, Jordan specializes in portraiture, event photography, and lifestyle shoots, working with clients ranging from individuals and families to corporate brands and creative agencies.

Jordan’s work is defined by a blend of natural light, candid moments, and refined composition—always aiming to create images that feel both personal and timeless. Whether it’s a quiet portrait or a bustling event, every shot is approached with care, precision, and a deep respect for the people in front of the camera.

When not shooting, Jordan can usually be found exploring the city with a vintage film camera or planning the next travel series.
